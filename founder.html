Donald Gordon---sponsor of Madison Academic Chess Club My background: General: Born in Milan, TN; Raised in El Paso, TX; Graduate of Milan High School and Memphis State University with degree in Mathematics; Worked in industry for 26 years in a chemical processing plant; Started teaching in 2009; Began teaching at MAHS in 2010; Interest in chess and games: My mother taught us to play card games, dominoes, checkers, and other board games from a very young age. I have never played a video game or stood in front of a video arcade game in my life. In high school my friends and I played Spades or Hearts every time we got together. Working in the factory I observed hourly employees rush to the break room to play Spades or Dominoes during lunch or break times. I have always appreciated the time spent playing games with friends.
